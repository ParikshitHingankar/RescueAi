Disaster Response Guides

🏔️ EARTHQUAKE RESPONSE

Magnitude-based response protocol activated

BEFORE
  • Identify safe spots: under tables, against interior walls
  • Secure heavy furniture to walls
  • Keep emergency kit ready (water, food, flashlight)
  • Know your building's evacuation plan
  • Save emergency contacts offline
DURING
  • DROP, COVER & HOLD ON immediately
  • Stay away from windows and glass
  • If outdoors, move away from buildings
  • Do NOT use elevators
  • If driving, pull over and stop
AFTER
  • Check for injuries, administer first aid
  • Inspect for gas leaks — don't use flames
  • Move to open areas away from structures
  • Expect aftershocks — stay alert
  • Report to RescueMesh AI via SOS

🌊 FLOOD RESPONSE

Flash flood emergency protocol

BEFORE
  • Move valuables to higher floors
  • Prepare waterproof emergency bag
  • Know your nearest evacuation route
  • Charge all devices, store offline maps
DURING
  • Move to highest point immediately
  • Never walk in moving floodwater
  • Avoid electrical equipment
  • Signal for help from rooftop
AFTER
  • Don't return until authorities clear
  • Avoid floodwater — it may be contaminated
  • Document damage for insurance
  • Disinfect everything water touched

🔥 FIRE RESPONSE

Structure & wildfire emergency protocol

BEFORE
  • Install smoke detectors on every floor
  • Keep fire extinguisher accessible
  • Plan & practice escape routes
  • Never overload electrical outlets
DURING
  • Activate fire alarm immediately
  • Stay low — smoke rises
  • Close doors to slow fire spread
  • NEVER use elevator — use stairs
AFTER
  • Do NOT re-enter building
  • Seek medical attention for burns/smoke
  • Report to fire authorities
  • Contact insurance immediately

🌪️ CYCLONE RESPONSE

Severe weather emergency protocol

BEFORE
  • Board up windows and doors
  • Stock 3-day water & food supply
  • Charge all communication devices
  • Identify nearest cyclone shelter
DURING
  • Stay in the strongest room of house
  • Do NOT go out during eye of storm
  • Stay away from windows
  • Monitor RescueMesh AI for updates
AFTER
  • Wait for official all-clear signal
  • Watch for downed power lines
  • Avoid floodwaters from storm surge
  • Help neighbors — report via RescueMesh

🌊 TSUNAMI RESPONSE

Coastal megawave emergency protocol

BEFORE
  • Know the tsunami warning signs
  • Identify high ground evacuation routes
  • Keep emergency kit ready
DURING
  • Move IMMEDIATELY to high ground
  • Do NOT wait for official warning
  • Go inland as far as possible
  • Never go to shore to watch waves
AFTER
  • Wait for official all-clear
  • Multiple waves may follow
  • Avoid damaged buildings

🚗 ACCIDENT RESPONSE

Road & industrial accident protocol

ASSESS
  • Ensure your own safety first
  • Turn on hazard lights
  • Call 112 emergency immediately
RESPOND
  • Do NOT move injured persons (spinal risk)
  • Control bleeding with direct pressure
  • Check for breathing & pulse
  • Keep victim warm & calm
AFTER
  • Document the scene safely
  • Exchange information with parties
  • Report to RescueMesh AI
